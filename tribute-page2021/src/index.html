J.K. Rowling

Author and philanthropist

JK Rowling is a British author best known for writing the world-renowned Harry Potter book series. Throughout her literary career, she has also published adult fiction novels under the pseudonym Robert Galbraith. Her books have been recreated into movies and television shows. The Harry Potter series is the top-selling book series of all time. (Wikipedia)
Due to her literary successes, Rowling accrued wealth worth over $1,000,000,000 beginning in the early 2000s. She regularly donates millions of dollars of her personal money to charities.
